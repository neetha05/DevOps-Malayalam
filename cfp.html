DMCon 2024: Call For Proposals (CFP)

Welcome to DMCon, the premier DevOps conference happening in the vibrant coastal city of Kochi, Kerala! Join us on July 27, 2024, for a day filled with innovation, learning, and collaboration within the DevOps community. This year marks the 2nd edition of DMCon, bringing together DevOps enthusiasts, developers, and industry professionals for a day of inspiring talks and invaluable networking opportunities.

Call For Proposals (CFP)

We invite proposals for Talks from DevOps enthusiasts. Submissions open on June 10, 2024, and close on June 30, 2024.

Talks

Technical talks are the heart of DMCon. The event will feature 30-minute sessions on topics related to DevOps, AI, and Cloud Native. Talks are selected through a CFP process, and an editorial panel will review and select the best proposals.

Important Points:

  • Talk duration:* 30 minutes
  • CFP closes on:* June 30, 2024
  • Schedule release:* July first week, 2024
  • Talk date:* July 27, 2024
  • Tracks:* 2 parallel tracks of talks in each session
  • Format:* Mix of lectures, demos, and Q&A

Proposal Submission Process:

  • 1.Submit your proposal via the CFP application.
  • 2.CFP volunteers review proposals for completeness.
  • 3.An expert panel reviews the proposals and provides feedback if needed.
  • 4.The panel votes on the proposals, and a pre-final shortlist is prepared.
  • 5.Shortlisted proposals go through rehearsals.
  • 6.The final list of selected proposals is published.
