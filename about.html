About DMCON-2024

Join us for DMCON 2024, the flagship annual event of DevOps Malayalam, on July 27th, 2024, in the vibrant city of Kochi!

Experience a premier event brimming with knowledge and excitement as we bring together DevOps enthusiasts, industry experts, and innovators under one roof. Whether you're a seasoned practitioner or just embarking on your DevOps journey, DMCON 2024 offers something for everyone.

Don't miss out on a day of cutting-edge discussions, diverse tracks featuring insightful sessions, and invaluable networking opportunities with over 300 attendees from across the DevOps community.
